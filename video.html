Vacant Vegas


I took a break from real estate aerial photography to get a drone view of the Vegas Strip during the covid-19 coronavirus pandemic lockdown in March 2020. The drone flights were cleared through McCarran Airport, so I took advantage despite a little rain!

It was very surreal, and I think this video captures that feel of awe and a bit of eerieness. Hopefully this pandemic lockdown in Vegas doesn't need to happen again in the future, but in the meantime enjoy this video of rare drone footage of the Las Vegas Strip!
