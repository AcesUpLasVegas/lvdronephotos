Recent Projects


Vacant Vegas


I took a break from real estate aerial photography to do some aerial videography and get a drone view of the Vegas Strip during the covid-19 coronavirus pandemic lockdown in March 2020. This was intended to be an aerial tour of Vegas and filming began right before lockdown measures were in effect. The drone flights were already cleared through McCarran Airport, so I took advantage dispite a little rain. This was mostly a free-style shoot and not focused too much on empty streets. I had footage of the Wynn and Venetian that did not make the video, as it was before the lockdown and looked out-of-place.

It was a fun project and I had some help along the way. There were so many other photographers out there during this time and people jogging or riding. It was very surreal, and I think this video captures that feel of awe and a bit of eerieness. Hopefully this pandemic lockdown in Vegas doesn't need to happen again in the future, but in the meantime enjoy this video of rare drone footage of the Las Vegas Strip.


The Lakes in Las Vegas
