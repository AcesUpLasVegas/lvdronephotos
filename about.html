Serving The Greater Las Vegas Area

Aces Up Drone

We provide quality aerial photo and video for your business or creative project. Our FAA Certified Drone Pilots are committed to a standard of excellence. Customer Service and Safety are top priorities for us. Working with a network of local photographers and videographers ensures that we can give you what you are looking for, and fast!

We specialize in Real Estate videos with quick turnaround time and competitive pricing. Also, consider hiring us for your Off-Road/Adventure photo shoot, Music Video, or Event! Hourly and daily rates available, will travel to remote locations upon request.
